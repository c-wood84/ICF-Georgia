About Us

We are the official Georgia Chapter of the International Coach Federation, the largest worldwide resource for business and personal coaches. We offer a supportive community, opportunities for professional development, networking, and an enjoyable way to connect with coaches from all backgrounds and specialties. We actively promote awareness of coaching to our communities. Curious about what coaching is and the impact it creates? Explore the ins and outs of the coaching profession to understand more about empowering others to achieve goals through coaching here.

Join us and experience the potential of coaching through career-enhancing, powerful programs and community involvement throughout the year. Via our virtual offerings, we are able to extend beyond Atlanta to offer benefit to our coaching professionals across the state. Our meetings & events are open to both members and guests.

We invite you to be a part of our vibrant and fun community of professional coaches–Let's learn, grow, succeed, and thrive together!

  • Vision

    In service of humanity flourishing, we support the global advancement of the coaching profession through impact and influence in our local community elevating personal and professional development for our local community members.

  • Mission

    As the Atlanta-based chapter of the International Coach Federation (ICF), the leading worldwide resource supporting the art, science, and practice of professional business and personal coaching, our mission includes four areas of strategic importance:
    • Member Involvement - Increase our membership and member involvement in the chapter volunteer experiences, meetings and events
    • Professional Development - Provide opportunities for members to engage in learning that expands professional accomplishment and credibility
    • Business Development - Help members grow their coaching practice through professional programs and speakers
    • Community Development - Raise the profile of coaching in the community through service work and advocacy

Welcome to the ICF Georgia Charter Chapter! We are the Georgia chapter of the International Coach Federation (ICF Global), the leading worldwide resource supporting the art, science, and practice of professional business and personal coaching. With more than 200 members, we actively support the advancement of the coaching profession by providing education, networking, and business development opportunities for members and our supporting community. Our members are entrepreneurs with their own coaching businesses as well as corporate professionals using coaching as a performance and development tool in their job at a local company. You don't have to be a professional coach to join ICF Georgia Charter Chapter. Business people and others who are interested in growing their coaching skills and learning more about the profession will find our high-energy meetings and speakers of great business value.  

  • Value Of Joining ICF Georgia

    Joining ICF Georgia as a Member or Affiliate will provide you with a springboard to educational and business success. Not only are you offered monthly educational opportunities at a discounted or free rate, but also leadership opportunities and the professional recognition for being a part of our organization, plus the networking opportunities available to you have proven to be instrumental for many to expand business opportunities and growth in the coaching industry.

  • Join Us!

    If you are a Global ICF member, you are eligible for our Member rate which includes discounted membership and extra benefits If you are not a Global ICF member, we welcome you as part of our coaching community as an Affiliate member. OUR MEMBERS ON THE BENEFITS OF BELONGING

If you are a professional coach in the Atlanta area, then you need to be a part of ICF Georgia. Continually improving our skills and deepening our learning makes us better coaches and ICF GA is a great place to do just that! Beyond that, the networking and potential partnerships that occur at each meeting are invaluable. It's a great way to stay connected to what's going on in the coaching community!

President and Founder, Ignite The Next International

If you want to be a professional coach, participate in your community of professional coaches." For me, it even goes beyond that. It is your responsibility to participate in your professional community. That's what the ICF GA chapter provides: a professional community of coaches where you learn together, grow together, support each other and advance our profession through the power of community. Welcome aboard!
